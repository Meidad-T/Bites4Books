Frequently Asked Questions

A little bit of background on why this exists! ❤️

Why are we doing it?

"I was thinking for hours - how can I make your journey easier?" and to me, sitting back and listening wasn't enough. so, I decided to make you the website with books as an incentive.

You love books, and I love your happiness and well-being, so its a win win!

Point system

every 200 points will be a book! I know it feels like a ton, BUT if you eat 3 meals a day, it's 1 book per week! should be pretty cool. In fact, there may also be a milestone bonus one day, so keep logging!

Tech Stack (are you a nerd? why would you read it?)

hehe anyways, this site is using plain HTML/CSS/JS and your data (points) is being handled by Firebase. Your Google Account sign-in is authenticated using Firebase as well.

The main reason I used firebase, is for saving your points so they are consistent across all devices in real-time, as well as being able to "award" you points through my admin power (I am cool right?).

Do I have to do this?

Not at all. you don't have to do it, I just wanted to try and provide an incentive to getting healthier, since I believe it is a journey you would go through anyways... might as well enjoy some books (alongside other support from me). But this is just a tool to make it go a bit easier is all...
